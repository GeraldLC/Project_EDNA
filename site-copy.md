## About Section
*(Full version for the About page)*

Jenisa Carvalho is a London-based Interior Designer with a First Class MA in Interior Architecture and Design from Middlesex University. Her work is guided by a simple belief — that great design should serve the people within it, without ever competing for attention.

With experience across residential projects, luxury kitchen interiors, and hospitality spaces, Jenisa brings both technical precision and a refined aesthetic sensibility to every project. She is fluent in AutoCAD, SketchUp, Enscape, and the Adobe Creative Suite, and has a track record of delivering work on time, on brief, and consistently above client expectations.

Before relocating to London, Jenisa worked in Mumbai where she designed luxury kitchens and managed high-end residential projects worth over $2M — developing her eye for detail, material quality, and spatial flow. Her academic research focused on inclusive and community-centred design, including award-level work on spaces for elderly communities and children with visual impairments.

She is currently based in London, open to full-time roles and freelance collaborations.
